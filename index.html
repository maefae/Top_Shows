    • Euphoria

    Written and directed by Sam Levinson, the HBO hit series follows a group of teenagers as they struggle through personal issues including drug addiction, identity, self-harm, and trauma.


    First Kill

    Have you ever enjoyed romance films such as Romeo and Juliette and Twilight? Well, First Kill is like the queer combination of the two. This American supernatural teen drama revolves around the lives of two girls; a vampire hunter, and a vampire. Unintenionally falling for each other, will they ever be able to find a way to stay together despite their entirely opposite upbringing?


    Ozark

    The final season of the hit series, Ozark, has everyone at the edge of their seats. A family must fight for their lives by laundering money for a mexican drug cartel.
